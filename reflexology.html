What is reflexology?

Think of your body as a tree, then our feet would be the roots of our body with thousands of nerve ending and pressure points being connected to a specific organ. Reflexology is a massage practice that focuses on the vital connection from our feet to the rest of our body, helping to increase energy level, blood circulation, and more!

What are the benefits?

Reflexology has been used since ancient times as a natural, non-invasive form of healing therapy.
